  \item \textbf{Sensitivity analysis}: Excluding the two preprint citations does not materially change any quantitative findings or conclusions. The preprints are cited only for specific experimental results that are corroborated by multiple peer-reviewed sources.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Preprint Citation Policy}

Our review included preprints under the following conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item The preprint reports original experimental data not available elsewhere
  \item The preprint is from a recognized server (arXiv, bioRxiv, medRxiv)
  \item The preprint findings are corroborated by at least one peer-reviewed source, or the finding is clearly marked as preliminary
  \item The preprint status is transparently disclosed (as in this supplementary file)
\end{enumerate}

This policy aligns with emerging systematic review guidance for including preprints in rapidly evolving fields such as AI and computational medicine~\citep{montezuma2025unbiased}.
